About Me

I'm Tharushi Fernando from Sri Lanka, currently an undergraduate student pursuing an Information Technology degree at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka. During my academic endeavors, I've delved into various fields to broaden my skill set. I have a keen interest in finance, demonstrated by my one-year internship as a trainee at the Regional Development Bank. Here, I honed my financial analysis skills, setting a foundation for my future as a Financial Analyst. Moreover, I've explored my creative side through a certificate course in graphic design with the National Youth Association in Sri Lanka. This experience allowed me to grasp the fundamentals of design principles and software tools, paving the way for a potential career as a Graphic Designer. Additionally, I am passionate about web development and layout design. Through my academic pursuits and extracurricular activities, I aspire to become proficient in these areas, potentially leading to roles as a Web Developer or Layout Designer. Overall, my academic journey and diverse experiences have fueled my ambition to continually enhance my leadership and language skills, preparing me for a dynamic and rewarding career path in the fields of finance, graphic design, web development, and layout design.
